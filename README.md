# MNQ PCS Regime Research

Research project to identify market regimes that may improve Friday MNQ put credit spread decisions beyond the existing VIX strike ladder.

## Primary objective

Determine whether trend, momentum, volatility, breadth, relative strength, price structure, or scheduled-event conditions materially change:

- VIX-ladder expiration failure rates
- Downside severity
- Failure clustering
- Maximum adverse movement
- Appropriate Friday trade treatment

## Possible trade treatments

- Normal entry
- Reduced position size
- Wider short-strike distance
- Skip the weekly cohort

No regime may change the operational strategy until it passes out-of-sample and walk-forward validation.
